Our Story

Behind the roastery

Corridor Seven Coffee Roasters (C7) is a premium specialty coffee brand and cafe chain headquartered in Nagpur. Founded by Q-Grader and barista champion Mithilesh Vazalwar, C7 is celebrated for its ethically sourced Indian beans, relaxed atmosphere, and signature artisanal brews.

Ethical Sourcing

A core part of our brand identity is our farm-to-cup supply chain, buying directly from Indian coffee farmers without middlemen to ensure exceptional bean quality and fair compensation.

The Vibe & Atmosphere

Featuring rustic, relaxed interiors with ample indoor and outdoor seating. It is engineered as a welcoming space for remote work sessions, deep reading, or slow weekend socializing.

Our Standout Roasts

While known for our technical pour-overs, crowd favorites include our refreshing "Spark It Up" cold brew, rich Iced Lattes, and a complete artisanal espresso range.

Where to Find Us

Visit our flagship destination in Sitabuldi (Bardi) or experience our cafe space in Dharampeth, Nagpur. Premium experiences crafted for true coffee enthusiasts.
